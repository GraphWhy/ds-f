Welcome to GraphWhy.org

For those who don’t know, GraphWhy.org is a registered non-profit in the state of california, This organization is loosely run by a group of bay area college students. Our mission is to educated to public through graphs and statistics.

This web address is currently being used as the organization's blog, and notice board. You can visit here to discover our thoughts, ambitions, newly released technology and, if you like, support the organization. While you are here we invite you to read our latest blog posts and participate in the linked surveys.

Notices & Updates:

  • GraphWhy.org is currently running on the DynamicStory infrastructure. Although the DynamicStory initiative is no longer active, we have repurposed the technology to our corporate blog.
  • The GraphWhy team is currently studying full time at some the Bay Area’s local colleges. Progress on the site will be very slow for the next year.
  • Our current ambition is to create a chatbot. We hope that use of this bot will allow our community to conveniently engage in GraphWhy Surveys.
  • If you would like to support GraphWhy the team has created merchandise that you will soon be able to purchase. All profits will go toward keeping the server online.
